1. OUR PRIVACY PRINCIPLES

We know Moonpair holds some of the most personal content two people share with each other — photos, voice notes, diary entries, private notes. The following principles guide how we handle it:

- Information you provide is private. We only use it to run the Service, and share it with your linked partner where the App is designed to do so.
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
- We do not use your personal information to advertise third-party products or services to you, and we do not run third-party ads inside Moonpair.
- We only work with third-party service providers under contracts that prohibit them from reusing or selling your data for their own purposes.
- We collect limited usage data to fix bugs and improve the App.

If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at support@moonpairapp.com.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Fnu Krish ("Moonpair," "we," "us," or "our") collects through the Moonpair mobile application and any associated website (together, the "Services"), why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights and choices available to you.

By creating an account or using Moonpair, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

3. TYPES OF DATA WE COLLECT

3.1 Account & Profile Data
Email address, password (stored in hashed/encrypted form via our authentication provider), display name, chosen "star," and answers to onboarding prompts (e.g., "what matters" to you).

3.2 Partner-Linking Data
The pairing code used to connect two accounts, and the resulting couple relationship record.

3.3 Content You Create ("User Content")
Photos and images, voice notes, diary entries, answers to relationship prompts (e.g., "Secret Book"), notes, gestures and messages sent to your partner, special dates, milestones, and memory descriptions.

3.4 Purchase & Subscription Data
When you subscribe to Moonpair Pro or purchase Stardust, the transaction is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details — only transaction metadata (product purchased, date, subscription status, renewal date) via our subscription management provider.

3.5 Usage & Device Data
Device type, operating system and version, app version, unique device/installation identifiers, general in-app usage and interaction data, and crash/error logs.

3.6 Push Notification Data
A device token used to deliver notifications to you via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM). You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

3.7 Communications
The content of any support request or message you send us.

3.8 Cookies (Website Only)
If you visit our website, we may use cookies or similar technologies (e.g., anonymous identifiers) to understand site traffic and improve the site. The mobile App itself does not use browser cookies.

4. INFORMATION ABOUT YOUR PARTNER

Moonpair is built for two people to connect. By design, certain content you create (photos, notes, shared memories, milestones, gestures) is visible to your linked partner, and content they create may reference you. Please only upload content and imagery you're comfortable sharing with your partner, and get their consent before uploading anything that includes them.

We do not knowingly collect information about anyone who is not a registered Moonpair user, except where a user includes it within their own content (e.g., a photo).

---

5. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use collected data to:
- Provide and maintain the Service, including account creation, partner pairing, and syncing shared content in real time
- Provide customer support
- Process subscriptions and Stardust purchases, and propagate Moonpair Pro access to both partners in a couple
- Send push notifications you've enabled
- Analyze usage to detect bugs, prevent abuse, and improve the App
- Comply with legal obligations
- Any other purpose with your consent

We do not use your personal content (photos, diary entries, voice notes, messages) to train AI/machine learning models.

---

6. RETENTION OF DATA

We retain your Personal Data and User Content for as long as your account is active. Usage/device data is generally retained for a shorter period, except where needed for security, functionality, or legal reasons.

If you delete your account:
- Your profile and login credentials are deleted or anonymized within 30 days.
- Content exclusively yours (e.g., a private diary entry not shared with your partner) is deleted.
- Shared content (joint memories, photos sent to your partner) may remain visible to your partner, since it forms part of the shared couple record, unless they also delete their account.
- We may retain limited data as required for legal, tax, fraud-prevention, or dispute purposes.
